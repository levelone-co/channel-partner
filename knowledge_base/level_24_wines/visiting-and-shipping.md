# Visiting and Shipping — Level 24 Wines

*Placeholder content. Replace with real estate details when this becomes a live account.*

## Tasting room

Open daily 10:00–17:00. Walk-ins welcome for groups of four or fewer; book ahead for larger parties via the contact form on the storefront. Tastings are R150 per person, waived with a purchase of two bottles or more.

## Shipping within South Africa

Standard courier (3–5 working days nationwide) via The Courier Guy. R95 flat fee per order; free above R1500. Wine is licensed for delivery to all SA provinces — no restrictions.

## Shipping outside South Africa

Currently shipping to Botswana, Namibia, and the UK via specialist export couriers. Lead time 10–14 working days. Quoted on request — contact the tasting room for export pricing on a specific order. We do not currently ship to the US, EU (excluding UK), or Asia.

## Returns

Corked or oxidised bottles are replaced free of charge — keep the cork and contact the tasting room within 14 days of receipt. Buyer's-remorse returns are not accepted.

## Payment

Card payments (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) via the Shopify checkout. EFT available on request for orders over R5000.
