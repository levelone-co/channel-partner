## Wine philosophy

Level 24's house style favours **restraint over flamboyance**: medium-bodied reds with bright acidity rather than blockbuster Cabs, and Chenin Blancs that age. Cellar-aged stock is held back for direct-to-consumer sales rather than supermarket distribution.

## Pricing posture

Mid-premium (R150–R400 retail), with the flagship Twenty Four range entering the premium tier (R350–R500). The estate does not chase the under-R120 supermarket segment.
